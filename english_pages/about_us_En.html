About us

The Production and Innovation Fund "Siberia" is a leading company specializing in innovation and the implementation of advanced technologies in agriculture. Our mission is to develop and introduce innovative agrotechnologies aimed at improving production efficiency, increasing crop yields, and reducing environmental impact.

We are actively working on creating sustainable and integrated solutions that contribute to the development of rural regions and ensure food security. Our company focuses on developing innovative farming methods, such as process digitalization, the application of precision agriculture technologies, and data management to optimize production.

The Production and Innovation Fund "Siberia" also actively collaborates with scientific and research institutions to continually improve and adapt new technologies to meet evolving climate and economic challenges. Our goal is to stay closely connected with advanced technologies and make them accessible to agricultural producers, fostering sustainable development and prosperity in the agricultural sector.
